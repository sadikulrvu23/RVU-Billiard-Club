Facilities

The University Billiard Club is proud to offer a comprehensive range of facilities designed to enhance the learning experience for individuals enrolled in our professional billiard course. Our state-of-the-art facility is equipped with cutting-edge billiard tables, each meticulously maintained to professional standards, ensuring an optimal environment for skill development. The club's professional course encompasses a structured curriculum led by experienced instructors who provide personalized guidance to participants at every skill level. The facility also includes dedicated practice areas, strategy rooms, and video analysis tools to offer a multifaceted approach to learning.
